Chicken Noodle Salad

Description

This recipe describes how to make a delicious bowl of chicken noodle salad. You can eat it cold, or you can dump it in a hot soup with fresh tomatoes. You can gulp it down, or you can feed it to the dogs. The possibilities are endless.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Noodle
  • Lettuce
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Place bowl on the table
  2. Add chicken.
  3. Add noodle.
  4. Add lettuce.
  5. Add spinach.
  6. Eat.
